About us

Who we Are

As one of South Africa’s 21 SETAs, the Energy and Water Sector Education and Training Authority (EWSETA) was established in terms of Section 9 of the Skills Development Act (SDA), 97 of 1998, as amended and reports to the Honourable Minister of Higher Education, Science and Technology. This portal aims to educate website visitors about the various careers in the Energy & Water sector in South Africa.

Psychometric Tests

Measure both cognitive ability and mental agility.

Are you a high school student struggling to decide which career path to take? With so many options available, it can be overwhelming and confusing. That’s where psychometric testing comes in. By taking a psychometric test, you can identify your strengths, weaknesses, interests, and personality traits, which can help you determine which careers are best suited for you. These tests provide valuable insights into your skills and abilities, giving you the knowledge and confidence you need to make informed decisions about your future.
